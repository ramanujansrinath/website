Drift Wallpaper

I am mesmerized by the Drift wallpaper in macOS. I tried to make something similar in Matlab. It uses 4D simplex noise for the lengths, orientations, and colours of the bars. (4D to make the rendered video loop seamlessly.) I have done similar things in Processing before and I would recommend that over Matlab for these kinds of things.
